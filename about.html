My Story

Kimball Mueller is a talented videographer, entrepreneur, and leader with a passion for creativity and making a positive impact. With five years of experience in cinematography, Kimball has mastered the art of storytelling through high-quality, cinema-grade videos that help businesses grow and succeed. From producing content that drives engagement to capturing meaningful moments through his wedding videography business, he combines technical expertise with a deep understanding of his clientsâ€™ visions. His commitment to excellence has earned him opportunities to work with a wide range of individuals and organizations, creating videos that inspire and resonate.

Raised in Utah and Idaho, Kimball was instilled with values of hard work, service, and community from a young age. As the eldest of nine siblings, he has embraced many opportunities to lead and serve, both within his family and in various personal and professional settings. A returned missionary from the France Lyon mission, he developed a strong connection to the French language and culture, which he continues to nurture through academic pursuits and international presentations. His time as a missionary strengthened his character and deepened his commitment to helping others, lessons he carries into every aspect of his life.

Kimball is also a dedicated student at BYU, where he balances rigorous academics with meaningful involvement in campus organizations. Whether excelling in courses like IS 201 or participating in service-driven initiatives through the Resident Hall Association (RHA), he approaches challenges with integrity and a drive for lifelong learning. His passion for skiing, music, and the outdoors complements his creative and adventurous spirit, and he has cultivated his musical talents over the years, mastering instruments like the piano, violin, cello, and ukulele.

With a unique blend of creativity, leadership, and a desire to uplift others, Kimball Mueller is focused on leaving a meaningful impact in everything he does. From his work helping companies grow to his personal pursuits of service and adventure, he is committed to making a difference in the world around him.
